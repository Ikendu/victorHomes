About Habit of Hope

Our Mission

At Habit of Hope Homes Inc., we’re dedicated to providing housing options and comprehensive support services to individuals overcoming homelessness, recovering from addiction, reentering from incarceration, and facing transitional challenges. Our mission is to empower our residents to rebuild their lives with dignity, stability, and hope for a brighter future.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has the opportunity to rebuild their lives and thrive in supportive communities.

Core Values:

- Compassion: We understand the struggles and challenges faced by individuals and families in need. - Integrity: We operate with transparency and dedication. - Empowerment: We strive to provide tools and resources for positive change.

Empowerment Through Education: Many organizations, like Home of Hope Inc. (HOHinc), focus on providing educational and vocational training to underprivileged and disabled youth. Programs in computer education, vocational skills, and K-12 schooling transform lives by giving students the tools to become self-sustaining adults. The habit of learning and consistent effort is the key to unlocking this potential. Building Life Skills for Independence: For adults with intellectual disabilities, groups like Homes of Hope (Illinois) focus on Community Integrated Living Arrangements (CILA). They offer round-the-clock support, person-centered planning, and assistance in developing crucial life skills—from household management to financial literacy. This cultivates the daily habit of independence, allowing individuals to thrive in their community. A Foundation of Housing: Stable, safe housing is the critical first step in building a hopeful future. Non-profits such as HOMES, Inc. (Kentucky) and Homes for Hope, Inc. (DC/SC) work tirelessly to provide affordable housing, especially after natural disasters or for those challenged with chronic illness and homelessness. By securing a stable environment, the habit of security allows clients to focus on personal growth and health..
